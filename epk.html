Artist Profile

Zhang Ruiyuan is a professional pop vocalist and stage performer currently based in Singapore. Trained at Communication University of China and Raffles Music College, she brings a unique blend of technical excellence and emotional authenticity to her performances. With expertise spanning pop, R&B, soul, and ballad genres, Zhang Ruiyuan has established herself as a versatile artist capable of delivering compelling performances across various settings.
